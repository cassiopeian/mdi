Finite Element Analysis

We offer state-of-the-art design optimization and analysis through finite element analysis (FEA).

Using FEA, we can help you better understand the limits of your concept, optimize the design, run virtual experiments, and perform stress/strain and fatigue analyses. We can prepare all the necessary documentation for regulatory submissions and answer any regulatory queries.

A Closer Look

Our analysis capabilities are not limited to any materials, and our expertise includes nitinol, polymers, and biomaterials.

Typical analyses that we perform include:

  • Nonlinear analysis
  • Nonlinear analysis with contact
  • Fatigue simulation
  • Biomaterial modeling
  • In vivo simulation
