Testing Services

We offer material and medical-implant testing services, to assist in the development and certification of your medical devices and implants.

Material Tests

Some of the tests that we perform on materials are:

  • Austenite finish (Af) of Nitinol
  • Tensile
  • Radial force

Cardiovascular, Peripheral, and Neurovascular Implant Tests

  • Pulsatile durability
  • Curved pulsatile durability
  • Bending durability
  • Crush durability
  • Modular junction durability
  • Radial force

ISO Compliance

We perform all the testing detailed in ISO 25539, which includes:

  • Dimensional verification
  • Simulated use
  • Balloon inflation/deflation time
  • Balloon fatigue
  • Stent diameter to balloon inflation pressure
  • Stent foreshortening
  • Flex/kink
  • Local compression
  • Dog-boning
  • Profile effect/flaring
  • 3-point bending
  • Profile/diameter
  • Force to deploy
  • Balloon rated burst (RBP)
  • Tensile bond strength
  • Torsional bond strength
  • Recoil
  • Crush resistance
  • Radial force
  • Dislodgement force
  • Stent free surface area
