About Us

MDI is comprised of individuals with a great passion for design, engineering, and manufacturing. This multifaceted enthusiasm is what drives us to bring functional, manufacturable medical devices to life, with precision and speed.

Our company was founded by engineers who have decades of experience in the medical device industry, and who understand the value of rapid iteration while evaluating a design's potential or improving on a design.

Whether we are designing, evaluating, or manufacturing a concept for you, we know that time is money, and we guarantee a swift turnaround without forfeiting the quality you need.
