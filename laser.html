Laser Services

We specialize in cutting complex shapes/designs from both tubing and flat stock comprised of an array of materials. As the implants get more intricate and the tolerances get tighter, we continue to upgrade our laser systems to meet the market demand.

Our lasers cut tubing that ranges in size from 0.008" OD through 0.750" OD, with walls as thick as 0.050".
